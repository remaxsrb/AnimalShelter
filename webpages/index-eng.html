Pet can prolong life

 Lazar Gospavic

 17-jun-2022

Researchers believe that people who live with animals are better able to cope with difficult situations in their lives, and that this is precisely because of the structured routine that is needed to take care of a pet. The next time your pet gets angry because of chewed shoes, his hair on your clothes, or if his barking wakes you up at night, remember that they could reduce the likelihood of heart attack and other fatal diseases.

Travel with a dog

 Marko Jovanovic

 15-jun-2022

Dog straps for fixing dogs in the car, are usable as harnesses and are suitable for all standard seat belt buckles. Not only is the risk of injury and accidents from animal companions very high, but the fines and associated costs are also high. Therefore: seat belts are valid for all passengers, no matter how many legs they have.
