About us

We are NADA!

Shelter "Animal hope" is a shelter for abandoned, healty or not, old and disabeled pets located in Belgrade.

Shelter was wounded 20 years ago and is register as an association for animal protection. It was founded by Marija Marković alone without any help and todat it is home to about 400 animals.

Even though it exists for more than 20 years, shelter is struggling on a daily basis and its biggest issue is food due to daily need of 100kg of food required to feed hosted animals.

Seconf issue is the winter which is yet to come, so animals need a warm place to hide from cold weather. Due to that very reason, a fund raiser was started to help the shelter. This website provided further help to the shelter in its intention to persevere.
